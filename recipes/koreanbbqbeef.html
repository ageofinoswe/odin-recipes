Korean BBQ Beef

Description

A yummy meal made by Chef Madeleine Ortiz.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onion
  • Brown Sugar
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Chili Flakes
  • Kewpie Mayonnaise
  • Sriracha Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare the bbq sauce.
  2. Marinate the beef in the bbq sauce.
  3. Cook the beef.
  4. Cook the rice.
  5. Chop green onions.
  6. Mix Kewpie Mayonnaise and Sriracha Hot Sauce together.
  7. Place rice in a bowl.
  8. Place beef on top of rice.
  9. Slather sauce on top of beef.
  10. Place chopped green onions on top.
  11. Sprinkle sesmae seeds on top.
