Sesame Noodles

Description

This wonderful dish created by Chef Alexander Fino.

Ingredients

  • Minced Garlic
  • Honey
  • Onion Crunch
  • Noodles
  • Sesame Sauce
  • Tajini
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Pasta Water

Steps

  1. Place garlic in large bowl.
  2. Mix in honey.
  3. Mix in soy sauce.
  4. Mix in onoin crunch.
  5. Mix in Sesame Sauce.
  6. Mix in Tajini.
  7. Cut and mix green onion in.
  8. Cut and mix cilantro in.
  9. Boil pasta to al dente.
  10. Drain pasta.
  11. Stir in pasta water to thin out sauce.
  12. Combine pasta and sauce.
